An unofficial portal to enable statewide searches for the Virginia Courts Case Information website.

Syntax: lastname, firstname
Syntax: partial_lastname
Example: Smith, John
Example: Smi
80 result max per court per division
Searches are cached for 3 days
While not supported by the statewide system, Virginia Beach is supported by this tool! Though the tool does not yet support partial name searches for Virginia Beach.
The Circuit Courts of Alexandria and Fairfax do not use the statewide Circuit Case Management System, and are not currently supported by this tool, though we hope to add support in the future.
